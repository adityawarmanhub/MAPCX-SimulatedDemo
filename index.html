Addressing Key Challenge 1: "False Ticket Resolution"

                
A deep dive into Pillar 2, moving from "Closed" to "Truly Resolved."

                

                    

                        
Problem

                        
Standard reporting tracks 'Closed Tickets' as a success metric, creating a major blind spot. This hides repeat contacts, inflates ticket volume, and erodes customer trust.

                    

                    

                        
Strategy

                        
Implement a scalable QA framework (Pillar 2) to measure "True Resolution" by analyzing customer sentiment and intent, not just ticket status.

                    

                    

                        
Action

                        
    
                            
  • Implement AI-driven QA to auto-score 100% of tickets for *negative sentiment*, even if the ticket status is "closed."
    • 
                            
  • Use keyword analysis to automatically flag high-risk tickets (e.g., "still not working," "frustrated," "I guess I'll wait") for human review.
    • 
                            
  • Integrate this data into the "Top 10 Failures" dashboard for targeted agent coaching on *resolution*, not just speed.

Addressing Key Challenge 2: Inefficient Queue Routing

                
A deep dive into Pillars 1 & 3, replacing FIFO with a "Skills-Based" model.

                

                    

                        
Problem

                        
A 'First-In, First-Out' (FIFO) model is highly inefficient. It treats simple WISMO queries and complex logistics failures with the same priority, increasing AHT and FCR.

                    

                    

                        
Strategy

                        
Deploy an advanced Intent-Based & Skills-Based Routing model, built on the foundation of my 3-Pillar framework to improve efficiency and agent satisfaction.

                    

                    

                        
Action

                        
    
                            
  • Pillar 1 (AI Bot): The AI triages the customer's *intent* (e.g., 'WISMO,' 'Refund,' 'Marketplace Issue') *before* it ever hits the queue.
    • 
                            
  • Pillar 3 (Team Tiers): The query is then routed *directly* to the agent with the right skills (T1 Generalist or T2 Specialist).
    • 
                            
  • This ensures complex issues are solved by experts on the first contact, bypassing unqualified agents and "swivel chair" escalations.
    • 
                        

                    

                    

                        
Projected Strategic Result

                        
15-Point Increase

                        
in First Contact Resolution (FCR) by connecting customers to the right expert the first time, every time.
